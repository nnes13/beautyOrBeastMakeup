About Me

Throughout my school years, I have always been known for my makeup skills and creativity. While attending high school, I started taking Special Effects Makeup classes at Pro Image Hair Academy in Barrie, earning one makeup certificate before I was finished high school.


I graduated from George Brown College Makeup School, receiving my full Makeup Artist certificate. During my training my teachers would highly recommend me for makeup jobs. Today, my makeup services and work ranges from Special Effects Makeup, Beauty, Glamour, Editorial, Photography, Bridal, Kids' Parties and more!
